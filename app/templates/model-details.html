Ethics Considerations

When using AI models in production, it's essential to consider the following ethical dimensions:

Fairness and Bias

Ensure your model treats all demographics and protected groups equitably. Models can inadvertently perpetuate or even amplify existing social biases if not carefully monitored.

Transparency and Explainability

Users and stakeholders should be able to understand how decisions are being made. Complex black-box models may require additional explanation mechanisms.

Robustness and Safety

Models should perform reliably even in edge cases and under adversarial conditions. Fragile models can make unexpected decisions when facing unusual inputs.

Privacy

Consider what data is being processed and how user privacy is protected, especially when handling sensitive personal information.
