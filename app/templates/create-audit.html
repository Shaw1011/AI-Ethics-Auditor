What Happens During an Audit?

When you start an audit, our system performs a comprehensive analysis of your AI model across three key dimensions:

Fairness Assessment

We evaluate how your model performs across different demographic groups, checking for disparate impact, statistical parity, and equalized odds to identify potential biases.

Explainability Analysis

We measure how interpretable your model's decisions are by analyzing feature importance, decision boundaries, and providing explanations for individual predictions.

Robustness Testing

We test your model's stability under various conditions, including adversarial inputs and data perturbations, to ensure reliable performance.

The audit process typically takes a few minutes to complete, depending on model complexity.
