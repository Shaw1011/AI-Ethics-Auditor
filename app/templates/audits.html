What are Ethics Audits?

AI Ethics Audits are comprehensive evaluations of machine learning models to assess their fairness, explainability, and robustness. These audits help identify potential biases and ethical concerns in AI systems.

Metrics We Analyze:

  • Fairness: Measures how equitably the model treats different demographic groups
  • Explainability: Evaluates how well model decisions can be understood by humans
  • Robustness: Tests how reliable the model is under various conditions and inputs
